FAQ- (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q-1.What is the application of derivatives?
Ans.Application of derivatives involves using derivatives to study rates of change, optimization problems, and the behavior of functions in mathematics and real life.
Q-2.Where are derivatives used in daily life?
Ans.Derivatives are used in physics, engineering, economics, business, medicine, and many other fields to analyze changing quantities.
Q-3.How do derivatives help in finding maximum and minimum values?
Ans.Derivatives help identify points where a function reaches its highest or lowest value, which is useful in profit maximization and cost minimization.
Q-4.Why are derivatives important in engineering?
Ans.Engineers use derivatives to design machines, structures, and systems by analyzing motion, force, and efficiency.
Q-5.How are derivatives used in physics?
Ans.Derivatives are used to calculate speed, acceleration, and other quantities related to motion and change.
