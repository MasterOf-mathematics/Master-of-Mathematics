FAQ- (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q-1.What is the application of trigonometry?
Ans. Trigonometry is used to measure heights, distances, angles, and directions in various fields.
Q-2.Where is trigonometry used in everyday life?
Ans. It is used in construction, navigation, architecture, astronomy, and computer graphics.
Q-3.Why is trigonometry important in engineering?
Ans. Engineers use trigonometry to design buildings, bridges, roads, and machines accurately.
Q-4.How does trigonometry help in navigation?
Ans. It helps determine location, direction, and distance for ships, aircraft, and GPS systems.
Q-5.How is trigonometry used in astronomy?
Ans.Astronomers use trigonometry to measure distances and positions of stars, planets, and other celestial objects.
