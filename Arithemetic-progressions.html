FAQ- (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q-1.What is an arithmetic progression?
Ans. An arithmetic progression is a sequence of numbers in which the difference between consecutive terms remains constant.
Q-2.Where is arithmetic progression used in real life?
Ans. It is used in salary increments, savings plans, loan repayments, and business calculations.
Q-3.Why is arithmetic progression important?
Ans. It helps identify patterns and simplifies calculations involving regularly increasing or decreasing values.
Q-4.How is arithmetic progression used in finance?
