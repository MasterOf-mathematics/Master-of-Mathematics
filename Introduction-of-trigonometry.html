FAQ- (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q-1.What is trigonometry?
Ans. Trigonometry is the study of angles and sides of triangles.
Q-2.Where is trigonometry used?
Ans. It is used in engineering, astronomy, navigation, and graphics.
Q-3.Why is trigonometry important?
Ans. It helps measure heights, distances, and angles accurately.
Q-4.Who uses trigonometry in real life?
Ans. Engineers, architects, pilots, and scientists use trigonometry.
Q-5.What does trigonometry improve?
Ans. It improves logical thinking and measurement skills.
