Probability

Probability in class 12 is a more advanced study of uncertainty and chance. It includes concepts such as conditional probability, random variables, and probability distributions. This topic is very important for higher mathematics, economics, statistics, and science.
Students learn how to solve complex probability problems involving multiple events. Probability is widely used in finance, insurance, business, and scientific research. Companies use probability to predict profits, losses, and future risks.
In medical science, probability helps study diseases and treatment results. Economists use probability to analyze investments and market trends. In technology, probability plays an important role in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analysis.
Probability for class 12 develops logical thinking and problem solving abilities. It prepares students for competitive exams and higher education. Overall, this topic has many practical applications in modern science and technology.
