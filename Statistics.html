Statistics

Statistics is the branch of mathematics that deals with collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data. It is widely used in business, economics, science, medicine, and sports.
Statistics helps people understand information through graphs, charts, and tables. Businesses use statistics to study customer behavior and market trends. Governments use statistics for population surveys and planning.
In sports, statistics help analyze player performance and match results. Scientists use statistics in experiments and research studies. Medical experts use statistics to study diseases and treatments.
Learning statistics improves data analysis and decision making skills. It helps students understand information clearly and make accurate conclusions.
