Surface Area and Volume

Surface area and volume are important concepts in geometry. Surface area measures the total outer area of an object, while volume measures the space occupied inside it. These concepts are widely used in engineering, construction, and architecture.
Builders use surface area and volume while designing buildings, tanks, and containers. In daily life, they help calculate paint, water storage, and packaging materials. Scientists and engineers use these concepts in designing machines and structures.
Surface area and volume are also important in manufacturing and transportation industries. Learning these concepts improves measurement and visualization skills.
Overall, surface area and volume are essential for solving practical problems involving shapes and objects.
