FAQ- (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q-1.What are the applications of integrals?
Ans. Integrals are used to calculate areas, volumes, distances, work, and many other quantities involving continuous change.
Q-2.Where are integrals used in real life?
Ans. Integrals are used in engineering, physics, economics, medicine, and environmental science.
Q-3.How do integrals help in finding area?
Ans. Integrals help determine the area enclosed by curves and boundaries accurately.
Q-4.Why are integrals important in engineering?
Ans.Engineers use integrals for designing structures, calculating volumes, and analyzing physical systems.
Q-5.How are integrals used in economics?
Ans.Integrals help calculate total profit, cost, revenue, and growth over a period of time.
