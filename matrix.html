Matrix

A matrix is a rectangular arrangement of numbers in rows and columns. Matrices are widely used in mathematics, engineering, computer science, and physics.
Matrices help solve systems of equations and perform complex calculations easily. Engineers use matrices in machine design, electrical systems, and structural analysis. Computer scientists use matrices in graphics, coding, and artificial intelligence.
In economics, matrices help analyze business data and financial systems. Scientists use matrices in physics and research studies. Matrices are also important in robotics and computer animations.
Learning matrices improves logical thinking and mathematical understanding. They simplify calculations and help solve real world problems efficiently.
